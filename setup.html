Device Setup

Install TeeeVeee on Your Device

Select your device below for step-by-step instructions

Amazon Fire Stick Setup

Works with all Fire Stick models including 4K, 4K Max, and Fire TV Cube

~3
minutes
1

Enable Apps from Unknown Sources

Go to Settings → My Fire TV → Developer Options and enable "Apps from Unknown Sources". On newer devices, look under Privacy Settings.

2

Install Downloader App

Open Amazon App Store, search for "Downloader" by AFTVnews. Install and open it.

3

Download IPTV App

In Downloader, type the URL for IPTV Smarters Pro or TiviMate. Click download and install when prompted.

4

Login with Xtream Codes API

Open the app, select "Xtream Codes API" login method. Enter your Server URL, Username, and Password from your welcome email.

5

Start Watching!

Your channels will load automatically. Browse categories, set favorites, and enjoy your content!

Android Setup

Works on all Android phones, tablets, and TV boxes running Android 5.0+

~2
minutes
1

Download IPTV App

Open Google Play Store and search for "IPTV Smarters Pro" or "Smarters Player Lite". Tap Install.

2

Open & Accept Terms

Launch the app and accept the terms of service to continue.

3

Select Xtream Codes API

Choose "Login with Xtream Codes API" as your login method.

4

Enter Your Credentials

Enter your Server URL, Username, and Password from your welcome email. Tap "Add User".

5

Enjoy!

All your channels and VOD content will load. Start streaming!

Pro Tip

For Android TV boxes, TiviMate offers the best interface with EPG support and picture-in-picture mode.

iPhone & iPad Setup

MAG Box Setup

MAG 250, 254, 322, 324, 420, 520 and all Infomir devices

~5
minutes
1

Get Your MAC Address

Go to Settings → System Settings → Network and note your MAC address (starts with 00:1A:79).

2

Send MAC to Support

Contact our support team via email with your MAC address to activate your device.

3

Configure Portal URL

Go to Settings → Servers → Portals. Enter Portal Name: TeeeVeee and the Portal URL provided by support.

4

Restart & Watch

Save settings and restart your MAG box. The portal loads automatically!

TiviMate Setup

The best IPTV player for Fire Stick, Android TV, and Android devices

~5
minutes

Install TiviMate on Fire Stick

1

Install Downloader App

Open the Amazon App Store on your Fire Stick, search for "Downloader" (orange icon by AFTVnews) and install it.

2

Enable Unknown Sources

Go to Settings → My Fire TV → Developer Options and enable "Apps from Unknown Sources" for Downloader.

3

Download TiviMate APK

Open Downloader, enter the URL https://shorturl.at/nrBS9 and press Go. The TiviMate APK will download (~11MB).

4

Install TiviMate

Click Install when prompted. After installation, select Delete to remove the APK file and save storage space.

5

Move to Home Screen (Optional)

Press and hold the Home button → select Apps → find TiviMate → press the Options button → select Move → move to top row.

Configure TiviMate with Your Subscription

1

Open TiviMate & Add Playlist

Launch the TiviMate app and select "Add playlist".

2

Select Xtream Codes

TiviMate will ask how to add a playlist. Select "Xtream Codes" option.

3

Enter Your Login Details

Enter your credentials from your welcome email:

  • Server URL: Your server address (from email)
  • Username: Your username
  • Password: Your password

Press Next to connect. TiviMate will load your channels and VOD content.

4

Name Your Playlist & Finish

Add a Playlist name (e.g., "TeeeVeee") and select Done. Your channels will appear in a minute or two.

EPG (Electronic Program Guide)

Our IPTV service includes a dedicated EPG. If you don't see the program guide, go to TiviMate Settings and enable the EPG option.

TiviMate Premium

TiviMate has a free version with basic features. For favorites, recording, and multiple playlists, upgrade to TiviMate Premium via the TiviMate Companion app from the Google Play Store. Note: TiviMate Premium subscription is separate from our IPTV service.
